About

The Townsend Firm is focused on data security and privacy. We help businesses navigate cybersecurity laws, respond to breaches, and stay compliant with regulations like GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA. Our goal is to protect your data and minimize legal risk in the digital age.

What We Do

We advise clients on data breach response, regulatory compliance, cybersecurity risk management, and privacy policy development. From GDPR and CCPA to HIPAA and emerging state laws, we help businesses stay secure, compliant, and prepared for the legal challenges of the digital world.

Costs

We offer flexible pricing options tailored to your needs, including hourly rates, flat fees, and subscription-based advisory services. Our goal is to provide transparent, cost-effective legal support that aligns with your risk profile and budget.
